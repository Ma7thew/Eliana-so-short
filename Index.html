Tribute - Terry Fox

Terry Fox, who is he?

Terry fox is symbol of hope for Canada. He started his run in Newfoundland and Labrador in April 12, 1980 and ran for 5,373 kms. In this Marathon of hope, he was able to raise $850 million since 1980 for cancer research.

Accomplishments:

  • Nearly ran a marathon a day for 143 straight days (5373 km)
  • He still ran with an artificial leg that was amputated in 1977.
  • Terry Fox raised $24.2 million dollars during his run.

Qualities of Terry Fox

The Important Qualities listed below are what allowed Terry Fox to push through and run for Cancer Research:

  1. Perseverance
  2. Sacrifice
  3. Commitment
  4. Passion
  5. Determination

Order of Countries he ran through

  1. Starting Point - Newfoundland and Labrador (St. John's)
  2. Nova Scotia
  3. Prince Edward Island
  4. New Brunswick
  5. Quebec
  6. Ending Point - Ontario
